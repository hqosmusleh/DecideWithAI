How It Works

DecideWithAI helps you make choices by providing AI suggestions. Simply type your dilemma above and get an instant recommendation.

Tips for Using

  • Be as clear as possible in your input.
  • Provide context for complex decisions.
  • Use this as guidance, not final advice.

About

This app uses OpenAI GPT-3.5 to generate suggestions for decisions. All processing happens securely on the server.

Example Scenarios

  • Choosing a meal plan for the week.
  • Deciding which movie to watch.
  • Picking a gift for a friend.
  • Prioritizing tasks at work or school.
